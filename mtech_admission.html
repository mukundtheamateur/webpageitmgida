Admission Overview

Fill your basic details and submit the form online. Print the application form, fill-in the required details completely, affix recent color passport size photograph and submit along with Xerox copies of:


  • Mark sheets of 10th/12th/Diploma/Graduation.
  • Pass certificate of 10th/12th/Diploma/Graduation
  • Certificate of Domicile (Other than U.P. Board)
  • Caste Certificate (in case of any reserved category)
  • Valid Family Income Certificate
  • Colored Passport Size Photographs (10)
